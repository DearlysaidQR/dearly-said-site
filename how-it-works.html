How It Works

Our QR-coded keepsakes are as easy as 1, 2, 3.

  1. Choose your card design — romantic, funny, or sentimental collections for every occasion.
  2. Upload your message — record a voice note or video, and we embed it into a custom QR code.
  3. We deliver the magic — your personalized card is printed, packaged, and shipped with love.
